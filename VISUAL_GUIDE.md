## 🎬 Animation Flow

### Page Load Sequence
```
Step 1: Background appears
  ↓
Step 2: Card slides up (0.5s)
  ↓
Step 3: Logo scales in (0.2s delay)
  ↓
Step 4: Text fades in (0.3s delay)
  ↓
Step 5: Form becomes interactive
```

### Form Switch Animation
```
Login → Signup
  ↓
Login slides left & fades out (0.3s)
  ↓
Signup slides in from right & fades in (0.3s)
```

### Theme Toggle Animation
```
Click icon
  ↓
Icon rotates 180°
  ↓
All colors transition smoothly (0.3s)
  ↓
Background changes with fade
```
